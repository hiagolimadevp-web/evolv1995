# EVOLV — Product Bible

The internal guide for every product, design, engineering and AI decision at EVOLV.
If a document ever contradicts this one, this one wins.

---

## Mission

We help people change their own lives.

We believe nobody can change another person's life.
But we believe the right tool, at the right moment, can give someone the courage to change their own.

That is why EVOLV exists.

---

## Vision

Become the world's most trusted personal evolution companion.

Not the biggest. Not the loudest. The most trusted.

---

## Core Philosophy

- Progress over perfection.
- Every small step matters.
- Consistency beats intensity.
- Respect before engagement.
- Science before trends.
- Honesty before marketing.
- Long-term trust over short-term growth.

---

## What EVOLV is

- A companion.
- A safe place.
- A daily partner.
- A guide.
- A system that helps people notice their own progress.

## What EVOLV is NOT

- A guilt machine.
- A motivation app.
- A habit dictator.
- A calorie obsession tool.
- A social comparison platform.

---

## Product Principles

Every feature must:

1. Help someone take the next small step.
2. Reduce anxiety instead of increasing it.
3. Respect the user's autonomy.
4. Never manipulate emotions.
5. Never create shame.
6. Feel human.
7. Feel calm.
8. Be simple.

---

## The Decision Filter

Before building ANY feature, ask:

1. Does this help the user take the next small step?
2. Does it reinforce our philosophy?
3. Would we proudly recommend this to our own family?
4. Does it make the user feel accompanied?
5. If we removed this feature, would EVOLV lose part of its identity?

If most answers are **No**, the feature should not be built.
If the answers are unclear, the feature is not ready. Wait.

---

## How we resolve conflicts

- **Growth vs. trust** → trust wins.
- **Engagement vs. wellbeing** → wellbeing wins.
- **Feature vs. simplicity** → simplicity wins.
- **Metric vs. human** → human wins.
- **Speed vs. respect** → respect wins.

We build for decades, not for quarters.
