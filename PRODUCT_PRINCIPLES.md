# EVOLV — Product Principles

EVOLV is not a fitness app. It is a personal evolution companion.

Our mission: **help people become a better version of themselves, one small habit at a time.**

Every design decision, screen, interaction and feature must reinforce this mission.

## The Ten Principles

1. **Progress over perfection.** A single step forward always matters more than a perfect plan.
2. **Encourage, never shame.** No red states, no guilt, no punishment — ever.
3. **Simplicity before complexity.** When in doubt, remove.
4. **Every feature must solve a real problem.** If it doesn't, we don't ship it.
5. **Every screen should answer:** *"What should I do next?"*
6. **Every interaction should make the user feel slightly better** than they did a second ago.
7. **Health and happiness are more important than numbers.**
8. **Celebrate progress.** Milestones, streaks, first steps — always acknowledged.
9. **Dreams motivate more than statistics.** We remember *why* the user is here.
10. **Build a product people love opening every day.**

## The tone

Calm. Beautiful. Premium. Encouraging. Friendly. Simple. Human.

Never a traditional fitness app. Always a trusted companion.

## Language rules

| Never say | Instead say |
| --- | --- |
| "You failed today's goal." | "Tomorrow is another opportunity. Every small step matters." |
| "Goal incomplete." | "You've made progress today. Let's continue tomorrow." |
| "You still need to lose 23 kg." | "You've already completed X% of your journey." |
| "No data." | "Your progress will appear here." |

## The one filter

Before adding anything, ask: *"Does this help the user become a better version of themselves?"*

If the answer is no, it does not belong in EVOLV.
